Praise Campaign App Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Praise Campaign app Privacy Policy. This document explains how we handle your data.

Our Commitment to Privacy

The Praise Campaign app was developed with a complete focus on user privacy and security. We value your trust and want you to know that we take your privacy very seriously.

Data Collection

The Praise Campaign app does not collect any personal data from users. This includes:

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or location.

No Tracking

We do not use cookies or similar technologies to track users.

No Sharing

As we do not collect data, there is no sharing with third parties.

Fully Offline Use

The app works primarily offline after installation.

Internet Usage

Updates

Internet access may only be required to download small app patches.

No Regular Communication

The app does not regularly communicate with remote servers and does not send user data.

Official Store Downloads

Updates are managed exclusively by official stores (Google Play and App Store).

Security

As there is no data collection, there is no risk of personal information leakage.

Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the app's practices. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.

The last update to this policy was made on: September 1, 2023.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how the app works, please contact us.
