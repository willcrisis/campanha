What is the Praise Campaign?

The Praise Campaign is a campaign created by the Monte da Santidade do Senhor church that focuses on systematically praising God for His attributes. The campaign follows a structured approach that spans 7 weeks, with each week dedicated to a specific aspect of God's character.

When the 7-week cycle is completed, it automatically restarts, becoming a continuous campaign of praise and worship.

Campaign Structure

The campaign is organized into 7 thematic weeks, each exploring a facet of God's character:

Week 1

The Love of God

Week 2

The Holiness of God

Week 3

The Majesty of God

Week 4

The Justice of God

Week 5

The Faithfulness of God

Week 6

The Mercy of God

Week 7

The Integrity of God

App Features

Automated

The app automatically determines which week and day of the campaign corresponds to the current date.

Biblical Texts

Displays the theme, attribute, and appropriate biblical verses for daily meditation.

Easy to Use

Intuitive interface that allows you to follow the campaign in a simple and practical way.

Shareable

Easily share the day's theme and verses with friends and family.
